## 2. String Harmonics

A guitar string is 64 cm long and has a fundamental frequency (one antinode) of 330 Hz. What is the speed of the wave on this string?

### Given:

- Length: $$L = 0.64 \,\text{m}$$  
- Frequency: $$f = 330 \,\text{Hz}$$  

---

### Fundamental Relation

$$
\lambda = 2L
$$

---

### Wavelength

$$
\lambda = 2 \times 0.64 = 1.28 \,\text{m}
$$

---

### Wave Speed Formula

$$
v = f\lambda
$$

---

### Calculation

$$
v = 330 \times 1.28 = 422.4 \,\text{m/s}
$$

---

### Final Answer

$$
\boxed{v \approx 422.4 \,\text{m/s}}
$$
