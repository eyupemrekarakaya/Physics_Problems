## 5. Echo Ranging

A person shouts towards a cliff and hears the echo 1 second later. How far away is the cliff? (Speed of sound in air is 343 m/s)

---

### Key Idea

Echo = gidiş + dönüş → süre iki kat yol içerir

---

### Formula

$$
d = \frac{vt}{2}
$$

---

### Calculation

$$
d = \frac{343 \times 1}{2} = 171.5 \,\text{m}
$$

---

### Final Answer

$$
\boxed{171.5 \,\text{m}}
$$
