## Wave Properties

Given:

- Frequency: $$f = 440 \,\text{Hz}$$  
- Speed of sound in air: $$v_{\text{air}} = 343 \,\text{m/s}$$  
- Speed of sound in water: $$v_{\text{water}} = 1482 \,\text{m/s}$$  

---

### Formula

$$
\lambda = \frac{v}{f}
$$

---

### Wavelength in Air

$$
\lambda_{\text{air}} = \frac{343}{440} \approx 0.78 \,\text{m}
$$

---

### Wavelength in Water

$$
\lambda_{\text{water}} = \frac{1482}{440} \approx 3.37 \,\text{m}
$$

---

### Final Answers

- Air: $$0.78 \,\text{m}$$  
- Water: $$3.37 \,\text{m}$$
