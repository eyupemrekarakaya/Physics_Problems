## 7. Standing Wave Modes

A standing wave with four antinodes is produced on a string of length L = 80 cm. What is the wavelength of this wave?

---

### Key Idea

n antinode → n. harmonik

$$
L = \frac{n\lambda}{2}
$$

---

### Given

- $$L = 0.8 \,\text{m}$$  
- $$n = 4$$  

---

### Calculation

$$
\lambda = \frac{2L}{n} = \frac{2 \times 0.8}{4} = 0.4 \,\text{m}
$$

---

### Final Answer

$$
\boxed{0.4 \,\text{m}}
$$
