## 11. Animation: Two-Slit Interference

Wave from two sources:

$$
u(\vec{r}, t) =
\frac{A}{|\vec{r} - \vec{r}_1|} \sin(k|\vec{r} - \vec{r}_1| - \omega t)
+
\frac{A}{|\vec{r} - \vec{r}_2|} \sin(k|\vec{r} - \vec{r}_2| - \omega t)
$$

---

### Parameters

- $\vec{r}_1, \vec{r}_2$ → slit positions  
- $d = |\vec{r}_1 - \vec{r}_2|$ → slit separation  
- $\lambda$ → wavelength  

---

### Path Difference

$$
\Delta r = r_2 - r_1
$$

---

### Interference Conditions

- **Constructive:**

$$
\Delta r = n\lambda
$$

---

- **Destructive:**

$$
\Delta r = \left(n + \frac{1}{2}\right)\lambda
$$

---

### Result

- Bright fringes → constructive interference  
- Dark fringes → destructive interference  

---

### Final Idea

$$
\boxed{\text{Interference pattern depends on path difference and wavelength}}
$$
