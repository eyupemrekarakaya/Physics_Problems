## 6. Wave Equation

A wave is described by:

$$
y(x,t) = 0.05 \sin(2\pi x - 50\pi t)
$$

---

### General Form

$$
y(x,t) = A \sin(kx - \omega t)
$$

---

### Identify Parameters

- Amplitude:
$$
A = 0.05 \,\text{m}
$$

- Wave number:
$$
k = 2\pi
$$

- Angular frequency:
$$
\omega = 50\pi
$$

---

### a) Amplitude

$$
\boxed{0.05 \,\text{m}}
$$

---

### b) Wavelength

$$
\lambda = \frac{2\pi}{k} = \frac{2\pi}{2\pi} = 1 \,\text{m}
$$

---

### c) Frequency

$$
f = \frac{\omega}{2\pi} = \frac{50\pi}{2\pi} = 25 \,\text{Hz}
$$

---

### d) Wave Speed

$$
v = f\lambda = 25 \times 1 = 25 \,\text{m/s}
$$

---

### Final Answers

- $$A = 0.05 \,\text{m}$$  
- $$\lambda = 1 \,\text{m}$$  
- $$f = 25 \,\text{Hz}$$  
- $$v = 25 \,\text{m/s}$$
