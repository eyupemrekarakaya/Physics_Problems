# Solution 07 — Logic & Series

## Problem

A bicycle is 10 meters from a wall and moves toward it at 1 m/s.

A fly starts from the bicycle and flies toward the wall at 2 m/s, then returns and repeats until the bicycle hits the wall.

Find the total distance the fly travels.

---

## Solution

Time for bicycle to reach wall:

t = distance / speed

t = 10 / 1

t = 10 seconds

Fly speed:

v = 2 m/s

Distance traveled by fly:

d = v × t

d = 2 × 10

d = 20 meters

---

## Answer

The fly travels:

d = 20 m
