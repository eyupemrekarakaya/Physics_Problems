# Solution 02 – Systems of Equations

## Problem

Find the values of x and y that satisfy:

2x + 3y = 12  
x - y = 1

## Solution

From the second equation:

x - y = 1

x = 1 + y

Substitute into the first equation:

2(1 + y) + 3y = 12

2 + 2y + 3y = 12

5y = 10

y = 2

Now find x:

x = 1 + y  
x = 1 + 2  
x = 3

## Answer

x = 3  
y = 2
