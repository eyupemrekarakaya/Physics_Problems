## 1. Series and Parallel Circuit

You have three resistors, 

$$R_1=15\,\Omega$$

$$R_2=30\,\Omega$$

and $R_3=50\,\Omega$ and a 12 V battery. Consider case when they are all connected in series and when all of them connected in parallel. Calculate the total equivalent resistance in each case. Calculate the current flowing from the battery in each case.
