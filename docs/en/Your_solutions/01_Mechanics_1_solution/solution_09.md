## 9. Momentum Comparison

Momentum is given by

$$
p = mv
$$

---

### Step 1: Convert masses to kilograms

Fly:

$$
m_1 = 2\ \text{g} = 0.002\ \text{kg}
$$

Tennis ball:

$$
m_2 = 60\ \text{g} = 0.06\ \text{kg}
$$

---

### Step 2: Calculate momentum

Fly:

$$
p_1 = (0.002)(10) = 0.02\ \text{kg·m/s}
$$

Tennis ball:

$$
p_2 = (0.06)(1) = 0.06\ \text{kg·m/s}
$$

---

### Step 3: Compare

$$
p_2 > p_1
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
p_{\text{fly}} = 0.02\ \text{kg·m/s}
$$

$$
p_{\text{ball}} = 0.06\ \text{kg·m/s}
$$

$$
\text{The 60-gram tennis ball has greater momentum.}
$$
